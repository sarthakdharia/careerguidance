About Bachelor of Commerce [B.Com] (Hons):

• Bachelor of Commerce [B.Com] Honours (Hons) is an undergraduate commerce course. • The duration of the course is 3 years in India. • It is generally divided into 6 semesters. • B.Com Honours subjects cover the study of principles of : o Accounting o Economic theory o Micro and macroeconomics o Money and banking systems o Knowledge of business o Trade and policies involved o The B.com Honours colleges that offer this course are:  Shri Ram College of Commerce  St. Xavier’s College  Hans Raj College, New Delhi etc. It is a career-oriented course and opens a wide scope of B.Com honors jobs after its successful completion. • This course is different from B.Com without Honors when it comes to the approach of learning where aspirants must pick the courses carefully as both the courses have different values in the market and offer a different level of opportunities Check: Courses After BCom

• Usually, it is Commerce stream students who choose B.com Hons course after 12th standard schooling. But technically, even Science, as well as Arts stream students, are eligible to pursue this course. • Candidates willing to apply for B.com Hons program must be 12th passed (10+2 Schooling system) with minimum 50% marks from a recognized board. • Since most of the colleges accept applications of candidates based on merit, few Universities require candidates to write an Entrance Exam for availing admission to the respective college. • Universities such as Delhi University, Christ University Bangalore, Banaras University etc are some of the B.Com Honors colleges in India which offer the course.
